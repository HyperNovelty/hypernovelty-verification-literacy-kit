# Open Lab Positioning

`hypernovelty-verification-literacy-kit` is the teaching and practice repo in the Hypernovelty Open Lab public footprint. It turns verification into short labs, templates, and discussion artifacts that can be used before automation touches public, paid, or sensitive surfaces.

It complements:

- `source-card-schema` for structured source and claim review.
- `agent-run-receipts` for documenting bounded agent-assisted work.
- `ai-workflow-safety-screen` for deciding when workflow review gates are needed.
- `ai-school-readiness-kit` for education-specific readiness checks.
- `hypernovelty-survival-kit` for the broader umbrella map.

## Public-Safe Boundary

The kit is educational and synthetic. It is not legal, financial, medical, cybersecurity, procurement, or compliance advice. Do not add private traces, credentials, source relationships, account workflows, client material, or public-action approvals.
