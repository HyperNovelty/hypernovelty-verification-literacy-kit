## Scenario

A school district is considering a small pilot of an AI study assistant for middle-school science homework. The assistant is not allowed to grade students, store student personal information, or replace teacher feedback.

A teacher asks an AI system:

> “Summarize what our pilot policy allows and identify one risk we should check before using the tool.”

## Evidence box

Synthetic policy excerpt:

> The AI study assistant may be used for optional homework practice in grades 6-8 science classes. It may explain concepts, generate practice questions, and suggest study steps. It must not assign grades, make disciplinary recommendations, collect student personal information, or be used as the only feedback channel. Teachers must review any generated study materials before sharing them with students. The pilot lasts six weeks and must include a parent notice.
