Mini-Lab Flow

  1. Give learners the worksheet before showing the answer key.
  2. Ask learners to score confidence for each AI answer from 1-5.
  3. Have learners inspect the evidence box and classify each answer.
  4. Ask for revised confidence scores after evidence review.
  5. Compare with the answer key and discuss what changed.

Materials

  • worksheet.md
  • answer-key.md
  • mini-lab README.md

Facilitator Prompts

  • What would you need to check before acting on this answer?
  • Which claims are supported, incomplete, plausible but wrong, or unsupported?
  • What changed your confidence after reading the evidence?
  • Where would a human review gate belong before public or sensitive use?

Answer-Key Boundary

Keep the answer key hidden until learners have recorded their first confidence score and classification. Use it as a discussion guide, not as a scoring authority for real-world claims.

Synthetic Example Note

This mini-lab uses synthetic, non-high-stakes material. It is not legal, financial, medical, safety, cybersecurity, procurement, or policy advice, and it does not verify real-world claims.
