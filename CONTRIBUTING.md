# Contributing

Contributions are welcome if they keep the kit practical, synthetic, and public-safe.

## Good contributions

- New synthetic verification exercises.
- Clearer worksheets or answer keys.
- Safer template language.
- Lightweight validators that operate on local files only.
- Accessibility and classroom-use improvements.

## Avoid

- Private source material.
- Real client/expert quotes without explicit permission.
- Account workflows, credentials, or logs.
- Claims that a template proves legal, financial, medical, safety, or compliance status.
- Vendor hype or benchmark claims without sources.

## Pull request checklist

- [ ] Examples are synthetic or clearly rights-cleared.
- [ ] No secrets/private paths/private logs are included.
- [ ] Claims are caveated and source-backed where needed.
- [ ] The change improves practice, not just terminology.
